Roasted Potatoes

Description

I love roasted potatoes and I wanted to pick 2 recipes that were really quick and easy since the butter chicken recipe was really long. This recipe is coming from the top of my head

Ingredients

  • 1 lb red potatoes cubed into half inch pieces
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Wash potatoes and peeling is optional
  2. Cube potatoes into half-inch pieces
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  4. Toss potatoes in oil and salt and pepper and place on sheet tray
  5. Roast in oven until desired browness is achieved
